About Me



Based in Essex, I am a copy writer and proof reader with a keen eye for detail, unwavering focus and the inability to take anything for granted.

I don't just proof read a document: I interrogates it. Armed only with a red pen, a dictionary and the absolute conviction that whatever I proof read, no matter how professionally produced, must contain at least one error, I contribute to the safeguarding of corporate, professional and personal reputations one word at a time.

I am just as at home working in-house as part of a publications or document-preparation team as I am working remotely, using whatever software or electronic methodology my client is most comfortable with. However you wish to work, I'm only too happy to accommodate.

'Never assume' is my motto. Anything that appears even slightly suspect will be comprehensively investigated. You're as likely to see me with a telephone in his hand, eliminating queries, as you are to see me grasping a red pen, poring over a printed page.

Although I am thorough by nature, I fully appreciates the hectic and demanding pace of the commercial world. I work swiftly and decisively, prioritising my workload to reflect my client's requirements. Neither pig headed nor pedantic, I recognise the necessity for intelligent and judicious compromise when deadlines loom and budgets demand.
